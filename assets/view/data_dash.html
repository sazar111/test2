Data Dash

RULES
Team Event
No of rounds: 2
Constraints: Exactly 2 Participants of same batch & year

BATCH-A:
Monday--Wednesday--Friday

BATCH-B:
Tuesday--Thursday--Saturday

ROUND 1: Entry level round

  • In this round, participants will be asked to solve logical puzzles and answer basic data analytics related questions.
  • Top scorers in this round will proceed to the finals.

ROUND 2: Final Round

  • II Years:
    • In the final round, participants will be given a dataset and a problem statement. The participants will have to derive insights from the given dataset. Points will be awarded based on the number of insights found. Those with the highest number of insights will be the winners.

  • III Years:
    • In the final round, participants will be given a dataset and a problem statement. The participants will have to implement the solution and based on the results obtained, points will be awarded to the students.
