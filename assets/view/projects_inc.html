Team Event
Eligibity: II , IIIYears.
Constraints:
Team of minimum 2 and maximum 3 from same batch and year.


Description: Ever dreamt of pitching your business solution to a company?

BATCH-A:
Monday--Wednesday--Friday

BATCH-B:
Tuesday--Thursday--Saturday


ROUND 1: Selection Round

  • Participants can choose their own problem statements
  • The overall project idea, abstract and the problem statement is to be presented to the judging team.
  • Selection Parameters: Idea presented, Technological Stack used, Approach in forming the solution

ROUND 2: Final Round

  • The participants will have to pitch the idea in front of the judging panel.
  • The judging team will judge the winners based on:
    • How far is the project developed?
    • The approach used for solving the problem
    • Presentation of the working model
    • Marketing Metrics (USP and Explanation of Marketability)
