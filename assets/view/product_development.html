RULES
Team Event
Eligibity: II, III Years.
Constraints:
Team of minimum 2 and maximum 3 from same batch and year.

Description:
Can you think out of the box and come up with innovative solutions? This is the right event for you techie, yes you!

BATCH-A:
Monday // Wednesday // Friday

BATCH-B:
Tuesday // Thursday // Saturday


ROUND 1: Selection Round

  • In this round, students will be tested on their computer fundamentals and product design skills.

ROUND 2:

  • The team will be given a problem statement to build a product.
  • This event will be a one day event.
  • The product can be anything: web application, mobile application, desktop application or any application.
  • The judgement will consist of various factors and there will be 3 judging rounds.
    • Round 1: How well the teams have understood the problem statement and how they plan to implement it?
    • Round 2: How far the product is developed and difficulties faced?
    • Round 3: Product Demo and Presentation.
